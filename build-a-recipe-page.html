Chatpate Recipie

It is the most heart warming dish among us Nepalese! No one can say no to Chatpate. It is very quick to make and tastes awesome and requires no cooking at all.

Ingredients

  • Puffed rice
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Boiled chickpeas or peas (optional)
  • Black salt accordign to the taste
  • Boiled potatoes (chopped)
  • Cucumber (optional)

Spices and Flavours

  • Green chilies (finely chopped)
  • Salt
  • Black salt (kalo noon) â€“ very important
  • Cumin powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Timur (Sichuan pepper) powder (optional but authentic)

Sauces & extras

  • Lemon juice
  • Mustard oil ( a very few drops )
  • Crispy noodles (wai wai, kurkure, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Put puffed rice in a bowl.
  2. Add chopped onion, tomato, boiled potato, chickpeas, green chili.
  3. Add salt, black salt, chili powder, cumin, timur, and a few drops of mustard oil.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice, add sauces if using, and mix well.
  5. Garnish with coriander, peanuts, or noodles and serve immediately.
